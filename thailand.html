Welcome to Thailand!

During my 8 weeks in Thailand i visited all of the most popular destinations. In this section i will be giving you a list of all the best places to visit and all the info you will need to ensure you have the best time during your travels.

Koh-Tao

With its pristine beaches, crystal-clear turquoise waters, vibrant marine life, and captivating culture, Koh Tao stands out as a must-visit destination for both adventurers and relaxation seekers alike.

Thing to do

  • Get Scuba Certified
  • Rent a motorbike
  • Check out all the amazing viewpoints
  • Enjoy the delicious seafood
    • Check out the best hostel in Koh Tao!

    Pai

    Pai is a charming town nestled in the mountains of northern Thailand. With a population of approximately 2,500 residents, Pai exudes a peaceful and laid-back atmosphere, making it a popular destination for both local and international travelers.

    Thing to do

    • Pai Canyon
    • Hot Springs
    • Visit all the amazing temples
    • Enjoy the delicious streetfood
      • Check out the best hostel in Pai!

      Ko Pha Ngan

      It is definitely worth visiting Koh Phangan, which has developed a reputation as one of the most relaxed tourist islands in Thailand. For travellers looking for the ultimate tropical escape, Koh Phangan offers beautiful beaches, authentic local culture, and wonderful accommodation options.
